The Lean methodology is a philosophy and mindset focused on driving maximum value to the customer while minimizing waste. These goals are accomplished through continuous improvement and respect for people.

I leaned heavily (pun intended) on [Trish Livingstone](https://www.linkedin.com/in/trish-livingstone-656ab714/) and [Julie Johnson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/julie-johnson-26a23213/) to help identify some of the benefits that an organization can hope to realize from Lean.

You can read about these benefits at [Geek Speak](https://thwack.solarwinds.com/t5/Geek-Speak-Blogs/ITSM-The-Four-Wins-of-Lean/ba-p/466605) and [<span style="color:orange">orange</span>matter](https://orangematter.solarwinds.com/2020/01/13/itsm-the-4-wins-of-lean/).
